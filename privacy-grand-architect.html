The Grand Architect

Privacy Policy — Last updated April 2026

Overview

The Grand Architect is a single-player idle game developed by Cole Hall. This privacy policy describes how the app handles user data.

Data Collection

The Grand Architect does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet connection, no account system, and no analytics. All game progress is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults system.

Third Party Services

The Grand Architect does not use any third party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Children

The Grand Architect does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
